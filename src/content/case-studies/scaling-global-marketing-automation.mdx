## The Problem

Twenty-three regional marketing teams. Fifteen different MarTech tools. Zero unified reporting. This Fortune 500 technology company had grown through acquisition, and their marketing infrastructure showed it.

**Every region was a silo.** Campaign data lived in spreadsheets. Lead scoring varied by country. The global marketing VP couldn't answer a simple question: "What's our pipeline contribution this quarter?"

## The Solution

We deployed a team of three marketing operations specialists—each with deep experience in enterprise MarTech unification:

### Platform Consolidation
Migrated 23 regions onto a unified Marketo instance with standardized templates, scoring models, and reporting dashboards.

### AI-Powered Lead Scoring
Implemented machine learning models that analyzed historical conversion data across all regions, creating a universal scoring framework that adapted to local market nuances.

### Automated Reporting
Built real-time dashboards connecting marketing activity to pipeline and revenue, giving leadership visibility they'd never had before.

## The Results

Within six months, MQLs increased by 200% as previously invisible leads surfaced through unified tracking. Campaign launch time dropped by 67%—regional teams could deploy global campaigns with local customization in days, not months.
