## The Problem

The mandate came from the C-suite: "Integrate AI into marketing operations by Q3." But the regional marketing teams—twelve of them across EMEA and APAC—had no AI expertise. No prompt engineers. No workflow designers. No one who understood how generative AI could actually transform their daily operations.

**Hiring wasn't an option.** The talent market for AI-fluent marketers was impossibly competitive, and traditional recruitment timelines would blow past the deadline by months.

## The Solution

We embedded three Human Change Agents (HCAs) across the regional structure, each bringing deep GenAI expertise combined with hands-on marketing operations experience:

### Custom Workflow Design
Built bespoke GenAI workflows for each region's specific needs—from automated brief generation to AI-assisted campaign optimization.

### Train-the-Trainer Programme
Developed a structured enablement programme that turned regional marketing managers into confident AI operators within six weeks.

### Governance Framework
Created an enterprise-grade AI usage policy and quality assurance framework, ensuring outputs met brand and compliance standards.

## The Results

Twelve regional teams went from zero AI capability to full operational independence in six weeks. Productivity increased by 290% as manual processes were automated. The C-suite deadline wasn't just met—it was exceeded.
